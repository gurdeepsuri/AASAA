About the studio

Two roofs, one idea — design that shelters life.

Founded in Bangalore, AASAA works at the meeting point of architecture and interiors. We believe the two are inseparable: a building's bones and its inner life should be imagined together, from the first sketch to the last detail.

Our practice draws from the city we work in — its climate, its courtyards, its craft traditions — and pairs it with a modern, restrained sensibility. Every project begins with listening, and ends with a space that feels unmistakably yours.

What we do

Services

Architecture

New builds designed for Bangalore's light and climate — residences, weekend homes and small commercial buildings, from massing studies to sanctioned drawings.

Interior Design

Interiors that balance warmth and restraint — space planning, custom furniture, material palettes and lighting, resolved down to the door handle.

Turnkey Execution

A single point of responsibility from design to handover — vendor coordination, site supervision and quality control, so you move into a finished space.

Renovation & Restyling

Reworking existing homes and offices — opening up plans, refreshing finishes and making older spaces feel current without losing their character.

How we work

Process

  1. 01

    Listen

    We start with your brief, your site and your way of living — no drawings until we understand all three.

  2. 02

    Sketch

    Concepts, plans and material moods take shape. We iterate with you until the idea feels right.

  3. 03

    Detail

    Working drawings, budgets and timelines — every junction and finish resolved before work begins.

  4. 04

    Build

    We stay on site through execution and handover, so what gets built is what was drawn.
